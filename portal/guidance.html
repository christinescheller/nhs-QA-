Official Transcript Requests

There are a few ways to request official transcripts and/or school records.

OFFICIAL TRANSCRIPTS CAN NOT BE E-MAILED

All Education Verifications requests should be faxed to 985.649.3613.

  1. You can come into the Guidance Department at the address listed on the website with your photo ID and pick up an official copy.
  2. You can email the “Request for Transcript” form that is on this website. Please include a clear copy of your photo ID and an address or fax number for the destination you want it mailed/faxed to.

Education Verification for Employment

  1. All requests need to be faxed to 985.649.3613
  2. Please include the following information:
    • Student’s full name-maiden name if applicable or other names used
    • Date of birth
    • Year of graduation
    • Last 4 of social security number
    • Fax number or mailing address to send results
