Welcome

At Northshore High School, everything we do is student centric. We act to create engaging experiences so that each student feels inspired, valued, and encouraged to aspire for success. Our goal is to put each student on a path to pursue whatever direction they may take beyond graduation, be that college or career.Â 

NHS is proud to offer our students a multitude of opportunities within 16 academic departments, 12 different sports, and over 30 clubs and activities. There truly is something for everyone at Northshore.
