Welcome to

Northshore High School

A leader in education since 1982, Northshore High School is a place where challenge, community, and creativity gives both students and faculty the skills to grow in wisdom and to better serve others.

Our NHS family strives to put our school motto into action every day: “Northshore High: a challenging and caring community encouraging lifelong learning for all students."

The faculty and staff at NHS prides itself on providing a challenging academic environment. For the third time in the last four years, Northshore High boasts the highest number of National Merit Semifinalists of traditional public school in the state of Louisiana. Last year’s seniors earned over in scholarships.

In addition to preparing our students to enter four year colleges, NHS students earn numerous certifications in areas such as computer programming, customer service, OSHA, ServSafe, and much, much more, which enables them to be career ready upon graduation. The well-known and award-winning NHS AG department, which boasts the largest chapter of Future Farmers of America in Louisiana, and the culinary department helps prepare students for careers in agriculture and culinary arts.

Northshore High provides a climate which encourages students to give back to their community. The caring students of NHS, through the ROTC program, have formed a partnership with our feeder school to help tutor students who are struggling academically. The ambassadors and Beta Club

Students from NHS are also involved with the award-winning Robotics team – who host an Astro Camp for elementary students – as well as the Foreign Language Club and exchange program that hosts . These activities afford opportunities to spark an interest in learning that will last a lifetime.

As an “A” rated school, the faculty of NHS is proud to serve its students and community.
